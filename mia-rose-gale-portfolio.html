About

I'm Mia.

I was born and raised in Marbella, Spain to British parents. I grew up around sand, sun and luxury. From a young age I was inspired by a lifestyle of freedom, choice and opportunity.

I went on to study Politics and Sociology at the University of Bristol. During my summers I interned in Bangalore, India, volunteered in Vietnam, and travelled across Thailand. These experiences gave me a deep appreciation for authentic storytelling across cultures.

Now I channel that curiosity and love of visual beauty into content creation, working with lifestyle, wellness, travel and digital brands to craft videos that genuinely connect.
